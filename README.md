# Flash ⚡
**The ambient signal protocol for high-speed, zero-interruption coordination.**
Flash is a communication layer designed for environments where attention is at a premium and every second counts. It replaces the high-friction "Notification-Action-Reply" loop with a **"Signal-Awareness-Handshake"** protocol.
Unlike traditional messaging apps that demand you read text or speak, Flash uses light, haptics, and spatial muscle memory to synchronize states between people instantly and soothingly.
## 📖 The Philosophy: "Perception over Reading"
In high-pressure environments—pharmacies, medical clinics, or busy commerce hubs—traditional notifications are "Active Interruptions." They force you to stop your task, process text, and formulate a reply.
**Flash is different:**
 * **Zero Social Debt:** No typing, no voice notes, no "seen" receipts.
 * **Ambient Awareness:** Information lives in your periphery. You don't "check" your phone; you perceive the state of a relationship through light and color.
 * **The Short-Pulse:** A middle ground between a text (too quiet) and a call (too loud).
## ⚡ Core Logic: The "Flash" Lifecycle
A Flash signal follows a precise mechanical path to ensure it is noticed without becoming a nuisance:
 1. **The Flash (0-8s):** The incoming signal "rings" for 8 seconds. The screen "blooms" with the sender's **Submerged DP**, accompanied by a unique haptic signature and a shimmering acoustic tone.
 2. **The Settle (Automatic):** If not immediately acknowledged, the ringing stops. The UI gracefully collapses into a persistent **Ambient Orb** on the Always-On Display (AOD).
 3. **The Pulse:** The Orb "breathes" with the color of the current state (e.g., Amber for waiting) until the handshake is sealed.
## 🕹️ Interaction: The "Binary Soldiers"
Flash ditches traditional text buttons for **Spatial Soldiers**—icons placed intentionally to leverage muscle memory.
| Phase | Left Soldier (Negative) | Right Soldier (Positive) |
|---|---|---|
| **Ringing** | ❌ (Decline / Ground) | ⚡ (The Bolt / Flash Back) |
| **Passive Orb** | ❌ (Decline / Clear) | ✔️ (The Checkmark / Got it) |
### The Melt Transition
Tapping a soldier triggers a **Melt Transition**. The icon (⚡ or ✔️) morphs into the solid color of the resulting state (Green for "Got it," Red for "Decline"). This provides a tactile sense of "sealing" a session, moving the task from your mental stack to the history log.
## 🖼️ Visual Identity
 * **Submerged DP:** Profile pictures are masked and recessed into the glowing Orb for rapid facial recognition.
 * **Chromatic States:**
   * 🟢 **Green:** Acknowledged / Got it.
   * 🔴 **Red:** Declined / Busy / Sealed.
   * 🟠 **Amber:** Waiting / Active Handshake.
   * 🔴 **Crimson (Pulse):** Critical / Urgent Escalation.

Flash is built with **Separation of Concern** at its core. The UI is a "dumb" reflection of a robust, local-first **State Machine**. Reliability is non-negotiable; a Flash must deliver with the same certainty as a physical tap on the shoulder.
