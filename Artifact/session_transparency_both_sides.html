Session transparency model showing both sender and receiver history with tap-to-expand timestamps

both sides hold the same record

sender sees

What I sent

Each ping time, escalation color, outcome. Their reply or absence of one.

receiver sees

What I received

Each ping time, what I replied and when. Exact timestamps. No dispute possible.

The record is symmetric. Neither side can rewrite it. If the receiver declined, it shows on both logs. If they never replied, both logs show silence — not a fabricated decline.

past sessions — tap to expand

Each entry in history is tappable. Collapsed view shows color + outcome. Expanded view shows the full ping timeline of that session.
