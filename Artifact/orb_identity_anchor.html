layer architecture — separation of concern

1 Dynamic glow Pulses and changes color based on session state. Completely separate from identity. Knows nothing about the person — only the ping count and state machine.
2 Frosted glass Backdrop blur that softens the glow into an ambient halo. Creates the "submerged" quality — the face feels inside the light, not on top of it.
3 Identity ring Thin border around the DP that inherits the current state color. Bridges the glow layer and the identity layer without merging their logic.
4 Quiet DP Static. Never animates. Only opacity and saturation shift with state. The anchor that holds still while everything around it breathes.

the soul of the product — what Nod actually is

Zero social debt

A ping is a transaction that closes itself. Got it — debt paid. Decline — debt respectfully refused. No lingering obligation. No unread guilt. The session ends and everyone moves on.

Presence without intrusion

You know the other person is there and active. But you haven't invited their voice into your headspace. The handshake is intimate without being invasive. That's a boundary most communication tools can't draw.

The silent call

Two people are briefly "on the line" — connected through vibration and light rather than sound and speech. The duration of a session is a shared moment in time. It starts, it resolves, it closes. Like a nod across a crowded room.
